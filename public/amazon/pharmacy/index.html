Amazon Pharmacy

Your medication history is sensitive data

Involved migration

What it is

Amazon Pharmacy is a mail-order pharmacy offering prescription delivery, often with significant discounts for Prime members and the uninsured. Acquired via PillPack in 2018. It has your complete prescription history, insurance information, and health conditions.

Honest assessment

Amazon Pharmacy's pricing is genuinely competitive, especially for generics. But your prescription history is among the most sensitive data you have — it reveals chronic conditions, mental health treatment, and health vulnerabilities. Amazon's terms permit using this data for advertising. Cost Plus Drugs (Mark Cuban's pharmacy) offers similar pricing with far better privacy practices.

What you lose

  • Prime prescription savings (many generics free or $1–5 for Prime members)
  • Convenient home delivery
  • Simple refill management

Data to export first

  • Request prescription history from Amazon Pharmacy customer service (1-855-745-5725)
  • Download your account information at privacy.amazon.com → Request My Data
  • Your prescribing doctor can also provide prescription history

How to transfer prescriptions

  • Contact your new pharmacy and give them your name, DOB, and current pharmacy info
  • They handle the transfer — you don't need to contact Amazon Pharmacy directly
  • Transfers typically take 24–48 hours
  • Confirm transfer is complete before canceling Amazon Pharmacy account

Alternatives

  • Cost Plus Drugs (Mark Cuban) — Transparent pricing — publishes actual drug costs + 15% markup. Often dramatically cheaper than insurance copays. Strong privacy practices. Best overall alternative.
  • GoodRx — Free discount card/app. Works at any local pharmacy. Often matches or beats Amazon Pharmacy pricing.
  • Your local pharmacy — Walgreens, CVS, Rite Aid, and independent pharmacies. Less convenient but keeps your data local and supports your community pharmacy.
  • Insurance mail-order pharmacy — Many insurance plans include mail-order pharmacy at 90-day supply pricing. Often free generics.
  • Walmart Pharmacy — Aggressively priced generics. $4 for many common generics. In-person option.

Migration steps

  1. Choose your new pharmacy
  2. Have your new pharmacy request the transfer (they initiate it, not you)
  3. Wait for confirmation that transfers are complete (24–48 hours)
  4. Request your full prescription history from Amazon Pharmacy before closing
  5. Close your Amazon Pharmacy account via customer service or account settings
  6. Delete Amazon Pharmacy data via privacy.amazon.com → Manage Your Data
