Subscribe & Save

Auto-orders you might have forgotten about

Moderate migration

What it is

Amazon's Subscribe & Save lets you set recurring deliveries on household goods, pet food, supplements, and more, with 5–15% discounts. Convenient, but also a mechanism that keeps you ordering from Amazon by default without thinking about it.

Honest assessment

Subscribe & Save is designed to make Amazon your default. The discounts are real, but many brands offer their own subscriptions with equivalent or better pricing, plus you build a direct relationship with the brand. Worth auditing what you're actually auto-buying.

What you lose

  • The discount (typically 5–15%)
  • Autopilot convenience for recurring purchases

Data to export first

  • Go to Amazon → Account → Subscribe & Save → Your Subscriptions
  • Screenshot or list every active subscription before canceling

How to cancel

  • Account → Subscribe & Save → Your Subscriptions → Cancel each item individually
  • Or cancel your entire Amazon account which terminates all subscriptions

Alternatives

Household Cleaning & Personal Care

  • Grove Collaborative — Natural household products. Auto-ship model. Often competitive with Amazon pricing.
  • Brand-direct websites — Method, Seventh Generation, Mrs. Meyer's all sell direct with subscriptions.

Pet Food & Supplies

  • Chewy — Autoship available. Often cheaper than Amazon. Exceptional customer service.

Natural Foods & Pantry

  • Thrive Market $60/year membership — Organic/natural at wholesale prices. Auto-ship available. Pantry staples category.

Supplements & Vitamins

  • Brand-direct websites — Most supplement brands (Garden of Life, NOW, Thorne) offer subscriptions direct at better pricing.
  • iHerb — Wide supplement selection. Auto-ship available.

Baby & Family

  • Grove Collaborative — Diapers, wipes, natural baby products.
  • Brand-direct — Pampers, Huggies offer direct subscription programs.

Migration steps

  1. Go to Account → Subscribe & Save and list every active subscription
  2. For each item, check if the brand sells direct or if a category-specific retailer covers it
  3. Set up replacements before canceling (Chewy autoship, Grove subscription, etc.)
  4. Cancel each Amazon Subscribe & Save item once the replacement is confirmed
  5. Set a calendar reminder to verify your new autoships are working after 30 days
