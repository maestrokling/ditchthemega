Audible

Your audiobooks stay — your subscription doesn't have to

Moderate migration

What it is

Audible is Amazon's audiobook subscription service ($14.95/month), the dominant platform in audiobooks with a large exclusive catalog. Good news: unlike many subscriptions, audiobooks you purchased on Audible remain accessible even after you cancel — you just lose the monthly credit.

Honest assessment

Audible has by far the largest audiobook catalog and the most Originals. Some titles genuinely aren't available elsewhere. But $14.95/month is steep if you're listening to fewer than 2 books per month — the library is usually the better deal.

What you lose

  • Monthly credit (1 credit = 1 audiobook, typically worth $15–40)
  • Audible Originals exclusives (some titles are Audible-only)
  • Whispersync (listen/read sync with Kindle)
  • Member discounts on additional purchases

Data to export first

  • Your purchased audiobooks remain in your library after canceling
  • To back up locally, use OpenAudible (free, open source) to download DRM-free versions
  • Note same legal gray area as Kindle DRM — personal backup, not distribution

How to cancel → Step-by-step guide on CancelFreely

  • Log in → Account Details → Cancel Membership
  • Amazon will offer a pause option (3 months) and credit bank reminder
  • Your purchased titles remain accessible after cancellation

Alternatives

  • Libro.fm — Best Audible alternative. Same price. Your purchase supports a local bookstore of your choice. DRM-free MP3 downloads.
  • Libby / OverDrive — Free audiobooks from your library. Wait times on popular titles but always worth checking first.
  • Chirp — Daily deals on audiobooks, often $1–4. No subscription. Great for deal hunters.
  • Downpour — DRM-free audiobooks. You actually own what you buy.
  • Speechify — Text-to-speech for any content. Different experience but worth knowing about.

Migration steps

  1. Check your credit balance before canceling — use any remaining credits on titles you want to keep
  2. Note your purchased library (Account → Library → Purchased)
  3. Cancel the subscription (your library stays accessible)
  4. Sign up for Libro.fm for future audiobook purchases
  5. Link your library card to Libby for free audiobooks
  6. Browse Chirp for discounted titles you've been putting off
