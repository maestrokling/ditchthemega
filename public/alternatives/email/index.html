Email Alternatives

Privacy-respecting alternatives to Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo Mail.

ℹ️ This page is affiliate-free. Recommendations exist because they're good, not because we earn a commission. The rest of DitchTheMega has disclosed affiliate links where they exist. Not here.

Email providers

Proton Mail Free tierFrom $3.99/month
Replaces: Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail Privacy: End-to-end encrypted; Swiss jurisdiction; Proton cannot read your email Platforms: Web, iOS, Android, desktop

Honest tradeoff: Occasional deliverability issues to servers that implicitly trust Gmail; small storage on free tier

Tuta Free tierFrom €1/month
Replaces: Gmail, Outlook Privacy: End-to-end encrypted including subject lines; German jurisdiction; post-quantum encryption Platforms: Web, iOS, Android, desktop

Honest tradeoff: Smaller ecosystem than Proton; contacts can't decrypt your emails unless they also use Tuta

Fastmail From $3/month
Replaces: Gmail, Outlook Privacy: Not end-to-end encrypted, but privacy-respecting; no advertising; Australian company Platforms: Web, iOS, Android

Honest tradeoff: Not encrypted at rest like Proton/Tuta; better UX than both
