Smart home platform

Home Assistant Free tierFree (hardware: $99 Green, $150 Yellow)
Replaces: Amazon Alexa/Echo, Google Home/Nest, Apple HomeKit Privacy: Open source; runs locally; your data never leaves your house; no subscription Platforms: Any device with a browser; native iOS/Android app

Honest tradeoff: Setup takes 1–2 hours; learning curve is real but front-loaded; much more powerful than Alexa once running

Cameras (Ring alternatives)

UniFi Protect Hardware: $100–400 NVR + cameras
Replaces: Ring, Nest cameras Privacy: Local storage only; footage never sent to a cloud server; no subscription required Platforms: Web, iOS, Android

Honest tradeoff: Requires a UniFi NVR or Dream Machine; higher upfront cost; best-in-class for privacy

Reolink $30–$100 per camera
Replaces: Ring, Wyze Privacy: Local storage via SD card or NAS; optional cloud (not required) Platforms: Web, iOS, Android

Honest tradeoff: Good value; cloud optional not required; less polished app than Ring
