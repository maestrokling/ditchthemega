Privacy Policy

DitchTheMega collects no personal data. We use no analytics. We set no cookies. We don't track your browsing. We don't know who you are. We don't want to know who you are.

The site is static HTML served through Cloudflare Pages. No server-side code processes your requests. No database stores your visits.

Some links on this site are affiliate links, clearly marked with an aff badge. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission. We do not track which users click which links beyond what the affiliate program provider tracks at their end.

If you email us, we receive your email. We don't add you to a list. We don't sell your address. We reply if a reply is needed and that's the end of it.

This site exists to help you take control of your data. We start by not taking any of it.
